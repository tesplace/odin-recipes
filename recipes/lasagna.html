Lasagna

Description

The delicious red stacked piece of Italian art; that constantly reminds of Garfield. It's easier than you think and we will guide you through all the process!

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 500 gr meat
  • 1 middle sized onion
  • Lasagna (Not fresh made of course!-would be better though)
  • 200 ml milk
  • 2 table spoon flour
  • 2 table spoon butter (melted)

Steps

  1. Start by adding finely chopped onions to the pan with fried oil.
  2. Add meat and cook 6-7 minutes.
  3. Add grated tomatoes
  4. Add seasonings and cook for 5 more minutes then set aside
  5. In another pan, melt the butter and add flour on top. Cook 4 minutes.
  6. Slowly add cold milk on top and stir it until it boils.
  7. Right before it boils add black pepper and salt.
  8. Spread the sauce to the oven pan and but lasagna on top and then meat.
  9. Repeat the process until the Ingredients are fully used.
  10. Put in a preheated oven of 180 degrees.
  11. Cook 20 minutes.
  12. Enjoy your meal!
