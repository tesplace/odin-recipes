Shakshukha

Description

The delicious fast paced dinner for those who are bit lazy
or just looking for something exotic!
Some say that this recipe comes from Turkish people!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 paprikas
  • Butter
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan and add the tomatoes and let them cook.
  3. Cut the paprika into small pieces.
  4. Add paprikas on top.
  5. Add seasonings.
  6. Put the eggs on top.
  7. Add the fresh parsley.
  8. And love your life!
