Tomato Pasta

Description

The "straight out of Italian Grandmother's Pan" recipe
that we collected for you from the poetical villages of eastern Italy

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Parmesan
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basilikum

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in a boiling water.
  2. Cook 11 minutes and then take the pasta out.
  3. Don't throw away all of the water. Put 200 ml aside.
  4. Slice the garlics and add them to the pan with fried oil.
  5. Add grated tomatoes on top and wait until it boils.
  6. Add fresh parsley.
  7. Mix the sauce an the pasta and cook 2-3 more minutes.
  8. After you put it into plates, add parmesan and 2-3 leaves of basilikum on top.
  9. Enjoy your meal!
